Who we are

Launched in 2024, Multytouch delivers comprehensive customer and technical support solutions designed to streamline your most critical yet resource-intensive operations. Our specialised services ensure your support infrastructure runs seamlessly, affording your core team the flexibility to concentrate on strategic initiatives.

Our Mission

We pride ourselves on investing significant time upfront to become subject matter experts in each client's specific domain by thoroughly understanding their product's features, benefits, target audience, competitive landscape, and unique value proposition. This comprehensive approach ensures we can speak authentically about their offerings and address customer inquiries with the same expertise and passion as an internal team member. Our commitment to deep product knowledge underpins our mission to represent our clients with the utmost professionalism and effectiveness in every interaction with their valued customers, maintaining the trust and reputation they've worked so hard to build.
